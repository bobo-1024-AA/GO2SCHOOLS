# GO2Schools - User Walkthrough

Welcome to **GO2Schools**! This guide will help you get started quickly and make the most of this platform to manage your child's admission journey.

## 1. Login & Setup
- **Quick Login**: Click "Login" on the home or profile page and use your Google account to complete registration.
- **Complete Profile**: On the "Profile" page, you can set your display name and choose a favorite avatar.
- **Switch Language**: If you prefer English, you can switch the interface to **English** at any time on the profile page.

## 2. Explore Schools (Finding Your Ideal School)
- **Enter Search**: Click the "Schools" icon in the bottom navigation bar.
- **Three-step Filtering**:
    1. **Select Level**: Click Kindergarten, Primary, or Secondary.
    2. **Select District**: Choose the district you are interested in from the 18 HK districts.
    3. **Select Category**: Choose types like Aided or DSS based on your budget or preference.
- **View Details**: Click a school card to see the detailed address and contact number.
- **Save School**: Click the heart icon, and the school will appear in your "Saved" list for future comparison.

## 3. Manage Admission Schedule (Calendar)
- **View Calendar**: Click the "Calendar" icon in the bottom navigation bar.
- **Add Schedule**: Click any date in the calendar, fill in the interview name, school, and time, then click "Save".
- **Cloud Sync**: All your schedules are automatically backed up to the cloud; you won't lose them even if you switch phones.
- **Edit & Delete**: Click an existing schedule card to modify content or click the trash icon to delete it.

## 4. Dashboard Features
- **Countdown**: The "Upcoming Dates" at the top of the home page shows how many days are left until important dates (e.g., allocation day, interview day).
- **Latest News**: Scroll down on the home page to read the latest EDB updates and admission strategy articles.
- **Quick Tools**: Click "Interview Dates" to quickly jump to the calendar; click "Parents Forum" to check upcoming community features.

## 5. Notifications
- **Red Dot Indicator**: When there are new schedule reminders or system updates, a red dot will appear on the bell icon in the top right of the home page.
- **View Notifications**: Click the bell to view all historical notification records.

## 6. Dark Mode Support
- This application supports system-level dark mode. When your phone switches to dark mode, the app interface will automatically adjust to eye-friendly tones for comfortable night use.
